HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your preferred Solana wallet from the App Store or Google Play. For desktop, install the Phantom browser extension from the official website.

Get Some ETH

Make sure your wallet has SOL to swap for $CHI. You can buy SOL directly inside Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or purchase it on a centralized exchange (like Binance or Coinbase) and send it to your Phantom wallet address.

Go to PUMP.FUN

Open Pump.fun using your browser. Connect your Phantom wallet when prompted.

Switch $SOL for $CHI

Once connected, paste the $CHI Contract Address on Pump.fun search bar. Swap SOL for $CHI. There are zero taxes, so no special slippage is required—just confirm and you're in.
